Features

Everything you need to sell on-chain

From storefronts to settlement — the platform handles the infrastructure so you can focus on your products.

⚡

Instant deployment

Spin up a fully branded marketplace in under two minutes — no smart contract expertise required.

🔒

Non-custodial payments

Buyers pay directly to your wallet. The platform never holds your funds — you stay in control at all times.

🌐

Custom storefronts

Your own URL, branding, and product listings. Customers see your brand — not ours.

📊

Live dashboard

Track sales, monitor transactions, and manage your inventory from a single clean interface.

🔑

Wallet integration

Built-in wallet generation and management. Each account gets a secure, isolated wallet out of the box.

🛡️

Fraud-proof by design

On-chain transactions are immutable and publicly verifiable — no chargebacks, no disputes.
